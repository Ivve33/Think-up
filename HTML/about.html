Built for students,

Think-Up was created to solve a real problem — Saudi university students lacked a single place to explore academic paths and collaborate effectively. We built the platform we always wished existed.

Our Mission

Empowering academic clarity

We believe every student deserves clear, accessible information about their academic journey — in their language, on their terms. Think-Up bridges the gap between universities and students through intelligent tools.

  • Bilingual content (Arabic & English)
  • AI-powered study session summaries
  • Real-time collaborative study rooms
  • Structured university & major explorer
